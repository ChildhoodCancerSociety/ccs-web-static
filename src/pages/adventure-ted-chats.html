Adventure Ted Chats is a guest speaker series that invites prominent individuals to share challenges/pivotal moments in their lives, the wisdom they’ve gained through those experiences, and how those challenges/moments brought them to what they’re doing today. Whether the challenges discussed pertain to childhood cancer, or the long journey to a dream career, each episode consists of an authentic conversation between a featured speaker and the Founder/CEO of Childhood Cancer Society, Tommy Head. This series is premiering for Childhood Cancer Awareness month (September 2023). Beginning September, be sure to tune in every Monday for a guest speaker’s trailer, and every Thursday to watch a new episode! Check out more about our upcoming guest speakers and each episode below:
