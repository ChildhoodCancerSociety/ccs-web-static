AI for Social Good Intern (Remote)

Responsibilities

Childhood Cancer Society invites you to join our dynamic team as an AI for Social Good Intern! In this role, you’ll be instrumental in amplifying the efforts across multiple departments, including Communications, Art, Development, and Video Production. Work alongside our superhero teddy bear mascot, Adventure Ted, and apply innovative AI technologies to boost our fundraising and awareness campaigns. Your creativity and AI expertise will be key in supporting our mission to assist families battling pediatric cancer. ✨

Collaborate with Communications, Art, Development, and Video Production teams to enhance their initiatives. Participate in Brain Trust meetings (Mondays and Thursdays at 8:30 PM EST). Develop and implement AI-driven content and tools for campaigns. Analyze data to improve campaign effectiveness and outreach. Innovate and propose AI solutions for philanthropic impact.

  • Passionate about AI, communications, philanthropy, and teamwork ❤️
  • Background in Computer Science, AI, Communications, or related field (technical skills a plus) ‍‍
  • Problem-solving, analytical, and creative thinking abilities
  • Strong communication skills and ability to work collaboratively ️
  • Eager to use AI for positive social impact

Desired skills

A strong interest in:

  • Gaining hands-on AI experience in a nonprofit setting
  • Contributing to a vital cause and make a real difference
  • Remote work flexibility
  • Networking with professionals in tech and nonprofit sectors
