ART TO HEART CAMPAIGN

Do you or someone you know have pediatric cancer? Does embarking on an Enchanted Theme Park Experience sound like a dream come true?

Then the Art To Heart campaign is just for you! With Art To Heart, we want to bring families an all-expenses-paid VIP Disney/Universal/SeaWorld Vacation with 5-star lodging for a week at Give Kids The World Village.

Check out the steps below to apply!

  • 1️⃣ Fill out an Adventurer Application and check off Art To Heart
  • 2️⃣ Draw Adventure Ted doing something you enjoy
  • 3️⃣ Submit sketch to ArtToHeart@ChildhoodCancerSociety.org
  • 4️⃣ Once receiving the above, a member of our team will contact you ASAP with additional details

We look forward to hearing from you!
