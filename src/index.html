About Us

Childhood Cancer Society

Childhood Cancer Society (CCS) is a volunteer-run 501(c)(3) non-profit dedicated to helping families who are struggling with childhood cancer. From financial support to providing gifts/wish grants, our efforts support these families to maintain a mindset conducive to healing.

We continue to help spread awareness and ultimately fulfill our mission through multiple initiatives: our TeddyGram Program, Adventure Ted Chats, Adventure Ted Chronicles, endless volunteering opportunities, and more!

My life’s work is to touch the lives of as many people as possible and to show those who are struggling that some things cannot be quantified—the power of hope is one of those things.

Tommy Head Founder / CEO

Our Mascot

Adventure Ted

Centered around the children’s book hero, Adventure Ted is "a brand of positivity created to help people overcome challenges.” Our content & social mission focuses on helping people to feel inspired and maintain an optimal mindset.

Adventure Ted is the official "spokes-bear" of the Childhood Cancer Society. He raises awareness and funds for the organization’s initiatives. All purchases made in our store are donated directly to CCS . Thank you for your help and support!

Volunteer Opportunities

Our range of internships, including roles in AI for Social Good, Animation, Communications, Graphic Design, Video Production, and Web Development, provides a platform for passionate individuals to harness their talents in a cause that truly matters.

Whether you're animating heartwarming stories, developing compelling communication strategies, or crafting engaging websites, your contribution will be a beacon of hope in the lives of those affected by childhood cancer.

Original Podcast Series

Adventure Ted Chats

Adventure Ted Chats is a guest speaker series that invites prominent individuals to share challenges/pivotal moments in their lives, the wisdom they’ve gained through those experiences, and how those challenges/moments brought them to what they’re doing today.

Whether the challenges discussed pertain to childhood cancer, or the long journey to a dream career, each episode consists of an authentic conversation between a featured speaker and the Founder/CEO of Childhood Cancer Society, Tommy Head.
